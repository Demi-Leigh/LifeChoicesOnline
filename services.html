Services

Since 2005, Life Choices has impacted over 200,000 lives with CHOICES and NOT CHARITY.
 

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Life Choices believes that the human spirit is remarkably resilient – no matter who our parents are, where we live, or what kinds of connections, abilities, and opportunities life may have offered us. Even in circumstances where the odds seem hopelessly stacked against us - there is endless potential waiting to be freed.

WE ALL HAVE THE POTENTIAL TO SHAPE OUR LIVES IN WAYS THAT WILL BRING US THE MEANING AND SUCCESS WE LONG FOR.

Our Model

There are five building blocks that can assist YOUTH to thrive.
 

Family Stability

Health

Education

Leadership Skills

Employment


By providing services in these building blocks to low income communities,
we assist youth to be on the pathway to fulfilling lives.

Our Work

We understand that no two people are the same, which means our interactive and constantly evolving interventions are designed to be holistic and to meet the needs of the individual. We believe in transformation from inside out.

This, in turn, creates a positive ripple-effect in the community.
